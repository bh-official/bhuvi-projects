New Technology Training Centre Opens in Leicester

A new technology training centre has opened in Leicester this week to help people learn digital and software development skills. The centre aims to support beginners and career changers by providing practical training in web development and IT.

Local officials said the centre will help improve job opportunities and encourage more people to move into technology based careers. Many students have already registered, and classes are expected to start this month.
